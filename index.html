Hey there, thanks for stopping by. I'm a Taiwanese-American graphic designer and illustrator. I also make websites (like this one!) and translate in my spare time. I’m especially passionate about typography, accessibility, and digital privacy. Some of my work and personal projects can be found below, and my resume can be viewed here.

I live in Seattle, USA with my partner and our two cats. When I’m not working, you can likely find me singing along to Sondheim or playing some online chess.

I’m currently available for hire! Drop me an email at hi@asjem.com to chat about your project or just say hello.
