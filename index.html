Hey there, thanks for stopping by. I’m a Taiwanese-American graphic designer and illustrator. I also make websites (like this one!) and translate in my spare time. I’m especially passionate about typography, accessibility, and digital privacy. I don’t use AI or any other kind of computer generation for my work, ever.

I live in Seattle with my cat Misha. When I’m not working, you can likely find me singing along to Sondheim or playing some online chess.

I’m currently available for hire! Drop me an email at hi@asjem.com to chat about your project or just say hello.
