Wedding announcement design

Custom design for a cross-cultural wedding

For her small wedding ceremony outdoors on a beautiful fall day, the Taiwanese client requested something minimal and modern, but still in touch with her cultural heritage. With that in mind, I used the Mandarin character “double happiness” — a cultural design element that conveys the happiness and unity of a marriage — on a red background, the traditional color for a joyous occasion.

I kept the colors, shapes, and fonts simple and clean, using flat floral elements and an autumnal palette to echo the wedding ceremony’s setting.
