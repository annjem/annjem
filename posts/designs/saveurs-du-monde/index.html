Saveurs Du Monde

Minimalist business card

For this conceptual fine-dining restaurant, I aimed for a minimalist, elegant quality. To that end, I limited the palette to dark gray and cream and kept the layout simple.

As the name suggests, the restaurant serves flavors from around the world. I used the clean lines of a topological map as a minimalist yet visually interesting way to echo that.

Role: graphic design, mockup creation

Deliverables: business card
