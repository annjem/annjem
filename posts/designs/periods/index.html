Periods Aren’t Dirty

Illustration for Planned Parenthood Ottawa

For this illustration used by Planned Parenthood Ottawa, my goal was to push back on stigma by presenting menstruation in an friendly, whimsical, yet extremely direct manner. I chose to depict several different products that people who menstruate can choose from, including tampons, cups, and pads.

Advertisements for menstrual products are often shown with a clear, window-cleaner-blue fluid — a complete departure from reality, underlining the stigma against menstruation. With that in mind, I intentionally used a bright red color throughout (with more muted yellows and blues as a contrast).
