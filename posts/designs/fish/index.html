No Such Thing As a Fish

Fan design of a whimsical poster

My unofficial poster design for the wonderful, irreverent, hilarious podcast No Such Thing As a Fish that often delves into the weirdest corners of science. I aimed for a whimsical aesthetic, and what better graphic than a colorful vintage illustration?

For the fonts, I went with the personality-packed Strange Times for the title and the delightful handwritten D.I.Y. Time for the smaller print. The organic quality of the two make them pair wonderfully well. The URL at the bottom is also set in Strange Times for better readability at small sizes.
