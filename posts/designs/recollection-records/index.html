Recollection Records business card

Retro business card with personality

For this conceptual record store’s business card, I wanted to create a design that instantly stood out. I chose the classic and evocative image of a cassette tape, plus a vintage, slightly faded color palette. The name of the store (set in the wild, personality-packed WC Mano Negra) is positioned as though it was scrawled across the “label”. I also applied a slight organic texture to help it blend better with the background texture.
