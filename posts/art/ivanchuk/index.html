My (attempted!) tribute to my favorite chess player of all time, the incomparable GM Vasyl Ivanchuk of Ukraine. His immortal victory against Garry Kasparov in 1991 is jaw-dropping, and if you’re at all interested in chess, you simply must see it. In fact, just go watch all his other games too!

(And fun fact: one of his most famous games was the basis of the final climactic match in Netflix’s The Queen’s Gambit.)
